Abstract

The MWV Tasks ontology (MWV-T) is a taxonomy containing OWL classes to classify **construction reparation tasks**, extending [cto:ReparationTask](https://w3id.org/cto#ReparationTask). The taxonomy is manually created based on existing PDF reports of Monumentenwacht regarding damage inspections. In its current state, it contains preventive and curative repair tasks regarding timber damage [1] and damage caused by insects [2]. The main taxonomy is structured using RDFS subsumption axioms.

The namespace for MWV-T terms is https://w3id.org/con-tax/tasks/mwv#

The suggested prefix for the MWV-T taxonomy is mwv-t

The Turtle version of the MWV-T taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/tasks/mwv/mwv.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the MWV-T taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/tasks/mwv/mwv.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the MWV-T taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/tasks/mwv/mwv.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the MWV-T taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/tasks/mwv/mwv.nt

The MWV-T taxonomy was created by Mathias Bonduel (KU Leuven) during his PhD research (2017-2020) funded by the Research Foundation Flanders in the form of a Strategic Basic Research grant (grant agreement 1S65917N).
